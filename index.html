Biography

Subrata Paul is bioinformatics scientist at the NIAID Collaborative Bioinformatics Resource (NCBR). His research interests include statistical genetics, statistical modeling, genomics including single cell RNA-seq, multiomics, whole genome sequencing, and survival analysis.
